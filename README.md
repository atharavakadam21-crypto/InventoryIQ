

# 🚀 InventoryIQ — AI Inventory Forecasting Platform 
(username: admin password : inventory123)

🌟 Project Overview

InventoryIQ is a modern AI-powered inventory management and business analytics platform designed to help businesses monitor inventory performance, analyze sales patterns, forecast future demand, and generate business reports through an interactive dashboard.

The platform combines Machine Learning, Business Intelligence, and Data Analytics into a single executive dashboard experience.

It allows users to:

* Monitor sales trends in real time
* Analyze inventory and stock performance
* Forecast future sales using AI
* Track revenue and estimated profit
* Detect low-stock products instantly
* Export downloadable PDF reports
* Interact with an AI-powered business assistant

InventoryIQ was developed to simulate a real-world SaaS analytics platform used by retail and inventory-driven businesses.



 📝 Project Description

The main goal of InventoryIQ is to solve common inventory management and business analytics challenges faced by retail businesses.

Traditional inventory tracking systems often lack:

* Predictive analytics
* Intelligent forecasting
* Interactive dashboards
* AI-powered insights
* Executive-level business reporting

To solve these problems, InventoryIQ integrates:

 📊 Business Intelligence

Interactive KPI dashboards and visual analytics.

🤖 Artificial Intelligence

Machine Learning-based future sales forecasting using Prophet.

📈 Data Analytics

Revenue analysis, category analysis, turnover metrics, and regional insights.

🧠 AI Copilot Assistant

A chatbot-like assistant that answers inventory and business questions.

 📄 Automated Reporting

PDF and CSV export functionality for business reports.

The dashboard was built using Streamlit for rapid deployment and interactive UI development.
